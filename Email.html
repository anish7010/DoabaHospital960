Feedback



The first thing to realise is that feedback in its broader sense is vital to life. Without feedback there could be no natural selection, no evolution. Feedback is an essential component of effective change. Within the workplace, there is always change. Paradoxically, change is perhaps the one constant. Feedback – seeking it, giving it and receiving it – is one of the most effective tools we have for harnessing and promoting change, so that it is to our benefit.
